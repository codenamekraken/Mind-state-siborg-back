Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Mind-state-siborg ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mental wellness and stress monitoring application using physiological sensor data, voice input, and facial expression analysis to provide stress insights and emotionally supportive recommendations.

1. Information We Collect

The app may collect and process:

  • Microphone Data: Voice analysis and stress detection.
  • Camera Data: Facial expression analysis.
  • Bluetooth Sensor Data: Heart rate, EDA, temperature, and motion data.
  • User Input: Responses and check-in text/audio context.
  • Device Information: Basic browser/device details for app operation.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Stress level detection and analysis
  • Providing AI-based supportive suggestions
  • Improving app performance and user experience
  • Generating session-level stress insight reports

3. Data Storage

Mind-state-siborg does not permanently store raw physiological data, audio recordings, or facial images by default. Data is processed temporarily unless a user explicitly saves report context.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share personal data with third parties for advertising or unrelated use. Data is used only for app functionality and analysis.

5. Permissions Used

  • Camera — facial expression analysis
  • Microphone — voice analysis
  • Bluetooth — physiological sensor connection
  • Internet — AI processing and report generation

6. Data Security

Reasonable safeguards are applied to protect data in transit and at processing time. HTTPS is required for production communication.

7. User Control

You may choose not to use camera, microphone, or Bluetooth features. You can stop app use at any time.

8. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this policy when features or compliance requirements change. Updates are posted on this page.
